# Introduction

This is the repository for the RoboCup @home team of the Ohm university of applied sciences.
Our goal is to participate in the @home league of the Robocup Federation at the RoboCup 2027 in Nuremberg.
With our industry partner Isento we strive to further research and development in the field of humanoid household robots.

# Description 

We are a student led robotics team based in Nuremberg at the university of applied sciences. 
As middleware we use ROS2, most of the software is written in C++ and Python including the Isento packages.

## Upper part
We use the (pib4s advanced 3D printed robot)[https://pib.rocks/de/pib-advanced/] as the upper part of our semi-humanoid robot.
As software backend for the upper part we use the isento backend as a foundation for our own packages.

## Currently working on:

### Hardware:
- Construction of the base (plan is to use an evocortex base)
- Subsequent integration of Lidars for 2D mapping
- Acquiring and integrating an capable computing unit
- Developing an onboard power supply with hot plug functionality
- Improving the gripper and/or the tendon actuators

### Software:
- Integration of the NAVStack and the SLAM Toolbox
- Audiovisual human machine interaction
- Optimizing kinematics of the arms
- Advanced Object Recognition
- Development of a state machin
